About

Host & Producer

Nicholas Wolf

Nicholas Wolf is the host and producer of Travels & Traditions. He took over the series from his father, Burt Wolf, and has continued its tradition of thoughtful, story-driven travel programming.

Nicholas splits his time between Jupiter, Florida, where the show is produced, and Dallas, Texas, where he is a student in the Investments and Alternative Assets program at SMU's Cox School of Business.

When not filming, Nicholas can usually be found researching the next story, testing a new restaurant, or planning a trip.

Creator & Original Host

Burt Wolf

Burt Wolf (1938–2026) was a journalist, author, and television host who spent five decades telling stories about food, travel, and culture. He produced over 600 episodes of television and won the first James Beard Award for Best Television Food Journalism. The New York Times described Travels & Traditions as “the best food, travel, and cultural history shows on television.”

Burt's work took him to more than 150 countries. He served as the food editor for CNN, authored multiple books, and built Travels & Traditions into the most watched travel series on public television.

His curiosity, warmth, and commitment to honest storytelling defined the show and continue to guide it today.
